## Industry-Specific Guidance: Restaurants

### Best Times to Visit
- **BEST:** 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM (between lunch and dinner rush)
- **GOOD:** 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM (before lunch prep gets intense)
- **AVOID:** 11:30 AM - 1:30 PM (lunch rush)
- **AVOID:** 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM (dinner rush)
- **AVOID:** Weekends (too busy)

### Best Days
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** Slower days, more receptive
- **Monday:** Often closed or recovering from weekend
- **Friday:** Prepping for weekend rush

### Tone & Approach
- **Lead with:** Customer experience ("I had lunch here and loved it")
- **Emphasize:** Online ordering, mobile experience, reviews
- **Avoid:** Technical jargon (they don't care about React or Next.js)
- **Show, don't tell:** Pull up the site immediately, don't just describe it

### Restaurant Owner Psychology
- **They care about:** Revenue, efficiency, reputation
- **They don't care about:** Code quality, hosting infrastructure
- **Pain points:** Wix limitations, no online ordering, poor mobile experience
- **Win condition:** Show them how the site drives more orders

### Proof Points That Matter
1. "60% of restaurant searches are mobile" (show mobile version)
2. "Online ordering increases average order size by 20-30%"
3. "Customers spend 80% more time on sites that load fast"
4. "Reviews drive 70% of new customer decisions"
